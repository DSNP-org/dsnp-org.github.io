The next generation of the internet

DSNP.org is an independent non-profit organization, focused on stewardship of the Decentralized Social Networking Protocol (DSNP). The organization fits within the umbrella of Project Liberty as the provider of one core component (the social graph protocol) that connects the many different technologies that make up a decentralized social network. Both DSNP.org and Project Liberty have been launched out of Unfinished Labs as part of their efforts to build public infrastructure for the web that puts citizens at the center of an innovative, inclusive web economy.
